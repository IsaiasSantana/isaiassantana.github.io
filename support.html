KeepSwipe – Support

KeepSwipe helps you clean up your photo gallery, one swipe at a time.

How KeepSwipe works

  • Review your photos in a large, simple card view.
  • Swipe or tap to keep or mark photos for deletion.
  • Use filters by date and size to focus on what you want to clean.
  • Check the delete list before removing anything from your gallery.
  • See storage usage, a donut chart by media type, and indexing progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos uploaded to any server?

No. All photo processing happens locally on your device. KeepSwipe does not upload your photos or videos to external servers.

Do I need an account to use KeepSwipe?

No. You can use KeepSwipe without creating an account. We do not ask for your name, email, or other personal data inside the app.

Why does KeepSwipe need access to my photos?

KeepSwipe needs full access to your photo library so it can display your photos as cards and allow you to delete items directly from your gallery.
